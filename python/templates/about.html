About Echo Radio

Listening to your favorite streams, anytime, anywhere.

Why I Built This

Echo Radio is a passion project built to help people Listen to their favorite radio stations in their local timezone. Built by an Englishman living in San Francisco who really missed waking up to his favorite UK radio shows.

How It Works

  • Streams are recorded and saved locally each hour.
  • The recorded audio is then played back in your chosen timezone.
  • Add multiple streams and play them back from a simple web interface.

Why "Echo"?

Because sometimes the delay from an echo is exactly what you need...
